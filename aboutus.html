About Us

Petit Gâteau was born from a simple passion for baking and a love of sharing sweet moments.

Our

Mission

Our mission is simple: create cakes that are just as beautiful as they are delicious. What began as a small love for designing pretty cakes quickly turned into a way of celebrating everyday moments. Every Petit Gâteau is crafted with care so people feel special when choosing them, sharing them, and coming back for more.

Our

Promise

We believe in using only the finest ingredients, combining traditional techniques with a modern touch, and creating an experience that feels warm and inviting. With each cake we make, our promise is simple: carefully curated flavors, attention to detail, and a little bit of magic in every slice.
